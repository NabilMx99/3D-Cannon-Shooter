This game consists of a cannon and targets. Whenever a cannon ball hits a target, the player's score increases by 1, and when the player misses the target, the player's score decreases by 1.

- To move the cannon use the keys "A" and "D".
- To launch/fire cannonballs, use the left mouse button.

This is my first Unity game ever. Feel free to give feedback on my game and i hope you enjoy it!. :)
